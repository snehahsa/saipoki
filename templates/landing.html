INTRO Welcome to the realm

SaiPoke Realm is a Pokémon-inspired social game inside Telegram. Create your trainer, explore a live multiplayer map, talk to NPCs, complete quests, and collect rare PokéCards from vending machines.

Battle other players for $POKECARD tokens in the group, track your rank on the leaderboard, and grow your vault. Every trainer starts with free coins to unlock their first avatar.
